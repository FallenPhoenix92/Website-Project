More About Me My Avatar

I am your basic gamer lad, I love RPG's mainly but I am prone to FPS's, Stratergies, and even a good Story game.

I have Autistic Spectrum Disorder, which basically means my head processes information differently and I often misunderstand alot of things. I also suffer with Anxiety and Moderate Depression so it's difficult some days but I try my best to make the best of things.

My Phoenix came about from World of Warcraft. back in the Cataclysm (4th Expansion) days, they introduced Guild Levels & Rewards and the Final Reward was a Dark Phoenix Mount whic I immediately fell in love with. I always called myself Phoenix in my games but I wanted something unique and cool, and a Fallen Phoenix was born!
